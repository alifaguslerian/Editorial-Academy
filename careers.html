Why The Editorial Academy?

We aspire to be as rigorous and visionary as the industries we serve, as inquisitive as the practitioners who use our platform, and as transformative as the future we strive to shape for everyone.

Learn more about our values →

Inclusive

When people feel supported, everyone benefits. We know that doesn't just happen — we're dedicated to making it a reality. We're building teams as diverse as the practitioners we serve, so we learn to see every discipline from every perspective.

Curious

Learning is who we are — inside and out. One reason we're so motivated to push editorial knowledge forward is because we recognize the power it has for us at The Editorial Academy. We never stop evolving, and neither do you.

Revolutionary

Why keep editorial knowledge to yourself? We'd rather share it with the people who need it most. We improve the careers of the practitioners and instructors we serve — using our expertise to empower theirs.

There are so many reasons our people choose to join us at The Editorial Academy — whether it's the mission, the culture, or the opportunity to grow.

Perks & Benefits

Of course, the best thing about being part of The Editorial Academy is knowing that your work elevates the practice of entire industries worldwide. We also have a great benefits package that makes life here even better — tailored to each of our team members.

Learning incentives

Full access to all Editorial Academy programs — plus a yearly budget for external professional development.

Learning programs

Our Learning & Development team provides top-notch training sessions and company-wide mastery initiatives throughout the year.

Holistic care

Mental health is vital. Access free coaching and therapy through our wellness programme and a dedicated mental health budget.

Charitable matching

Support causes that matter to you. We match your donations up to an annual limit for eligible nonprofits and charities.

BEDI program

We prioritise diversity and inclusion through equitable hiring, retention, mobility, and leadership development across every team.

Guest speakers

We regularly host speakers from across the media and design industries to enrich our team's perspective and continuous learning.

Company events

Enjoy team celebrations, cultural events, wellness activities, and extracurriculars like editorial salons and creative workshops.

Global Leave Programs

A global parental leave policy that applies to all new parents, inclusive of gender, family structure, and path to parenthood.

Parental benefits

We support fertility, adoption, and family-building expenses with comprehensive coverage and dedicated family health resources.

Financial care

We support your financial wellbeing with access to financial planners, equity participation, and long-term planning resources.
